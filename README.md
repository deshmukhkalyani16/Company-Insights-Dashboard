# 📊 Company Insights Dashboard

## 📌 Overview

The **Company Reviews & Job Market Analysis Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI project that provides insights into company reviews, job opportunities, salaries, interview statistics, industries, and locations. The dashboard helps users analyze employment trends and compare companies using dynamic visualizations and KPIs.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze company review data.
- Track job opportunities across different companies.
- Compare salary and interview statistics.
- Identify industry-wise employment trends.
- Explore location-based job distribution.
- Create an interactive dashboard for business insights.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

- **Power BI Desktop**
- **Power Query**
- **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)**
- **Microsoft Excel**
- **Data Modeling**

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

- ✅ Total Reviews KPI
- ✅ Maximum Jobs KPI
- ✅ Highly Rated Companies Analysis
- ✅ Salary Analysis
- ✅ Interview Analysis
- ✅ Industry-wise Salary Distribution
- ✅ Benefits by Industry
- ✅ Location-wise Job Analysis
- ✅ Company-wise Interactive Filtering

---

## 📊 Key KPIs

| KPI | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Reviews Count | Total number of company reviews |
| Maximum Jobs | Total available job postings |
| Total Salary | Salary comparison across companies |
| Total Interviews | Interview statistics |
| Highly Rated Companies | Count of highly rated companies |
| Benefits | Industry-wise employee benefits |
